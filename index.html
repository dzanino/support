--- ### 👋 About me I'm a forensic pathologist based in Slovakia. In daily autopsy and expert-witness work I kept reaching for the same calculations again and again — so I started building small, reliable tools that put them all in one place. I care about clean design, evidence-based methods, and software that simply works, offline, when and where it's needed. ### 🧰 Featured project — 4n6 Šikuta Tools · *Forensic Hand Toolkit* A pocket web app (single-file, installable to the home screen) with a growing set of forensic-medicine calculators. Runs **entirely on the device** — nothing is uploaded or stored. **▶︎ Try it: [dzanino.github.io/4n6](https://dzanino.github.io/4n6/)**  ·  **Code: [github.com/dzanino/4n6](https://github.com/dzanino/4n6)** What's inside: - **Thanatology** — time of death by Henssge's nomogram; post-mortem interval from vitreous potassium (Sturner, Madea, James, Zilg) - **Forensic entomology** — illustrated blow-fly development timeline (egg → adult) with a degree-day PMI estimate - **Biochemistry** — Traube index (glucose + lactate) for ante-mortem glycaemia - **Cardiac pathology** — body surface area and sex-specific predicted heart weight - **Identification & anthropology** — age from aortic circumference; stature from long bones (Trotter & Gleser); fetal age from diaphyseal length - **Anthropometry** — BMI and nutritional status - **Toxicology** — ethanol interpretation (blood/urine/vitreous, Widmark) and a reference table of therapeutic / toxic / lethal drug concentrations ### 🧪 Principles - **Offline-first & private** — works without internet; no data leaves the device - **Light / dark / system theme** — comfortable contrast in any lighting; fingerprint-themed UI - **Evidence-based** — every method is cited from peer-reviewed sources - **Orientational by design** — a decision-support aid; final judgment always rests with the examining physician ### 📫 Contact - Email: **jansikuta@me.com** © 2026 Ján Šikuta, MD, PhD.
